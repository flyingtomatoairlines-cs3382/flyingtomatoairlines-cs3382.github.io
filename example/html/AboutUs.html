Wanting to try some traditonal Japanese food but don't know where to find? We are here waiting for you! Don't hesitate!

Japanese Barbecue(Yakiniku) is the most famous dish in our resturant. We use the pork and beef shipped from Kobe, and cooked them by dry distillation(sumibi), which gives you a taste of nature. Customer is recommended to enjoy it with the special sauce made by us.

After finishing the main dish, customer is recommended to try the handmade chocolate to finish the whole dining. You can choose the one with matcha-favor, toffee-favor or strawberry-favor.

If you don't love chocolate as dessert, no problem! We have alternative choice for you. Sweets with matcha or nut are waiting for you!
