Company History

Flying Tomato Airlines --- a fruitful journey starts here!

Found in 2015, Flying Tomato Airlines is a Hong Kong based LLC (Low Cost Carrier) which aims to give a sweet taste to our passengers --- in low price and with popular destinations!

Milestones

Oct 2015: Start operating

Nov 2015: Flights to Japan in service

Jan 2016: Flights to Korea in service

Apr 2016: Club Pilot Tomato was established

May 2016: Flights to Thailand in service

Jun 2016: 50,000 passengers had travelled with us!

Jul 2016: Flights to Vietnam in service

Oct 2016: 1st Anniversary!
